12.34 Cake Design

  • Bookmark
  • Add to a term

Requirement:

None

Course Level:

Undergraduate

Cakes are baked and eaten. We will look at the ways in which a cake fulfills its role as a delicious tasty treat. Different flavors and sizes are discussed. Covers chocolate, vanilla, and lemon cakes. No previous cake knowledge is needed. Enrollment is limited.
